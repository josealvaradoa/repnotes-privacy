# Privacy Policy

**RepNotes AI**  
**Effective Date:** January 19, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

## Introduction

RepNotes AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (if using email sign-up)
- Apple ID identifier (if using Sign in with Apple)
- Display name you provide during setup

### Profile Information
- Training goals
- Available equipment
- Experience level
- Unit preferences (lbs/kg)

### Workout Data
- Workout text entries you create
- Exercise names, weights, sets, reps, and other metrics
- Workout timestamps and durations
- Personal records

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system version (for app compatibility)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the app's functionality
- Parse and structure your workout entries using AI
- Sync your workouts across devices
- Track your progress and personal records
- Personalize AI parsing based on your equipment and training context
- Send workout-related notifications (rest timer alerts)

## AI Processing

RepNotes AI uses artificial intelligence to parse your workout text entries into structured data. Your workout text is sent to our servers for processing. We do not use your workout data to train AI models.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform
- Data is encrypted in transit using TLS
- Authentication is handled through Supabase Auth with industry-standard security practices
- We implement row-level security to ensure you can only access your own data

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete your account and associated data at any time
- Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Supabase** — Authentication and database hosting
- **Anthropic (Claude API)** — AI-powered workout text parsing
- **Apple** — Sign in with Apple authentication

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your workout data
- Opt out of optional data collection

## Children's Privacy

RepNotes AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

**Email:** support@repnotesai.com

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

## International Users

If you are accessing RepNotes AI from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.
